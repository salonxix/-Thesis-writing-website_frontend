Additional Insights

A literature review is a crucial component of any academic research project. It entails examining, critically assessing, and synthesizing the existing body of literature related to your research topic. The aim of a literature review is to:

  • Offer an in-depth summary of the existing knowledge on your topic
  • Highlight deficiencies or gaps in the current research
  • Demonstrate how your research addresses a previously identified gap to validate its significance
  • Showcase your comprehension of the field and your capacity to critically evaluate pertinent information
  • Develop a theoretical framework to support your own research
