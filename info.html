THE QUALITY OF A GOOD THESIS

Contestable & An Arguable Point

We are committed to educational sectors with more than ten years of experience. Our core mission is to help working professionals complete their studies/research work. We love to assist them in every single step of the way.

Guide The Readers

It provides readers with a map to guide them through your work because a subject of a research paper is the general content. A topic is the specific issue being discussed. It is engaging at the same time!

It Tackles A Subject

It tackles a subject that could be adequately covered in the format of the project assigned. A subject of a research paper is the general content. Subjects are broad and general. For example: Health, Television, Travel.

Counter Arguments

It anticipates and refutes the counter-arguments. Writing a thesis is making an: you propose a thesis and offer some reasoning, using evidence, that suggests why the thesis is true.

Specific And Focused

It is specific and focused. A strong thesis proves a point without discussing "everything about". The topic of a research paper, in contrast, is the specific issue being discussed.

Avoids Vague Language

It avoids vague language like "it appears". Not too much Passive voice! All standard elements of grammar should be in moderation. Language should be clear, unambiguous and objective.

Asserts Conclusions

It clearly asserts your own conclusion based on evidence. We are flexible here. The evidence may lead a scholar to a conclusion you didn't think you would reach. It is perfectly okay and we can help to change your thesis!

It Avoids The First Person

It avoids the first person, like "I believe," "In my opinion". In some fields, writing in the first person (i.e., using the words "I", "me" and "my") is strongly discouraged.
